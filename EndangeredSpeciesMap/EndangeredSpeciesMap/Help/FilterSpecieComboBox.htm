Filter Species Combobox

Filter Species Combobox allows user to filter all species that already exist in the application.

User can filter species by different categories:

  • Critically Endangered
  • Endangered
  • Vulnerable
  • Dependent on the habitat
  • Close to risk
  • Smallest risk
  • Isolated
  • Partially habituate
  • Habituated

When user filters species by selected category, results will be displayed in the tables of species below.
